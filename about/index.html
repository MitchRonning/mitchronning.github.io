About

By admin on 06 December 2011 .

Our goal is to help YOU get back to what YOU love to do! We offer one-on-one patient care, delivering hands-on physical therapy based on current evidence AND years of clinical experience.

All physical therapists at Ronning Physical Therapy are licensed and have extensive post graduate training. Our highly skilled staff is dedicated to performing individualized physical therapy procedures and modalities to help you regain independence, mobility, and strength. Ronning Physical Therapy- The Physical Advantage!

We promote patient education, empowering our patients to take their health and wellness into their OWN hands. We provide manual therapy, exercise, core stabilization, functional activity training, sports specific techniques, preventative care, pain control modalities AND most importantly- getting YOU back to YOUR life!
