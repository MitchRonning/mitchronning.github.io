  • Jennifer Murphy, MPT

    Jennifer Murphy, MPT, graduated from Samuel Merritt College in 2000 with a Masters in Physical Therapy. Quickly thereafter, she enrolled in a year long intensive Manual Therapy course with John Seivert of Grass Valley. When Pilates became a critical adjunct in her practice, she completed her Pilates training and became certified by The Pilates Method Alliance in 2005.

    As an undergraduate, Jennifer received her Bachelors degree from Stanford University majoring in Human Biology. While at Stanford, Jennifer was co-captain of the Women’s Track and Field Team and earned two time All-American Honors. She now enjoys chasing her two small children while keeping balance between her Pilates practice, running and swimming.
