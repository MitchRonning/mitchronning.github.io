Why does RPT get the best results?

Individual Care

One-to-one care ensures your comprehensive evaluation, treatment, exercise prescription, and home program are provided by a licensed physical therapist on an individual basis.

Manual Therapy

Utilizing hands-on manual therapy techniques based on the current best research evidence for patient success.

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Evaluation and treatment for surgical and non-surgical orthopedic injuries.

  • Neck- Whiplash, muscle strains, worn joints, nerve compression, disc problems, and bone spurs.
  • Shoulder- Rotator cuff, frozen shoulder, referred pain, bursitis.
  • Back- Muscle strains, sciatica, disc disease, stenosis, spondylolisthesis, arthritis.
  • Upper Extremity- Tennis Elbow, tendonitis, carpal tunnel syndrome, fractures.
  • Lower Extremity- Hip and knee arthritis, bursitis, ligament injuries, chondromalacia, ITB syndrome, plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendonitis, bunions.
  • AND MORE!

Sport Specific Rehabilitation and Injury Prevention

Evaluation and treatment of sports injuries, with rehabilitation including appropriate exercises for return to sport and injury prevention.

Body Mechanics and Posture Training

To inform and instruct our patients on proper positioning, posture and body mechanics to assist with symptom management, relief, and prevention.

Orthotic Fitting

20 years of experience with custom foot orthotics.

Ergonomic Assistance

to help control and prevent pain and overuse symptoms.

SPORTSMETRICS

A nationally recognized scientific approach to decreasing the risk of knee injuries in young athletes.
