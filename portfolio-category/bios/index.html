  • Mitch Ronning, PT, OCS

    Mitch is the owner and chief physical therapist at Ronning Physical Therapy. Born and raised in Santa Cruz, California, he received an undergraduate degree in Health Science from California State University Northridge and his Physical Therapy degree from California State University Northridge/ University of California Los Angeles.

    In 1995, he became the first physical therapist in Santa Cruz to be recognized by the American Physical Therapy Association as a board certified clinical specialist in Orthopedic Physical Therapy. His continuing education credits include the “Pacific Manual Therapy Year Long Course” and the “Santa Cruz Orthopedic Manual Year Long Program”.

    Mitch enjoys treating patients of all ages but has a special interest in youth sports. He is a former youth coach in football, soccer, baseball, softball and wrestling. In 2011, he completed specialized training in SPORTSMETRICS- a nationally recognized program featuring a scientific approach to decreasing the risk of anterior cruciate ligament injuries.

  • Ginny Keely, PT, DPT, OCS, FAAOMPT

    Ginny joined RPT as a staff Physical Therapist in 2005. She earned her Doctorate in Musculoskeletal Physical Therapy from Evidence in Motion (http://evidenceinmotion.com/) in 2012, her Master of Science in Physical Therapy from Duke University in 1990, is Board Certified in Orthopedic Physical Therapy, and is a Fellow in the American Academy of Orthopedic Manual Physical Therapists (http://www.aaompt.org/), the culmination of a 3-year post-professional training program specializing in manual orthopedic physical therapy.  She provides detailed assessment of primarily orthopedic injuries and dysfunction, but most enjoys foot/ankle, hip and sports-related conditions.  Prior to returning to her Central Coast roots, she spent 11 years as full-time orthopedic faculty at Texas State University.  She is an avid cyclist, and performs bike-fitting services within the local cycling community. She also enjoys hiking, plays guitar, and can often be found walking her dog, Keeper, on Carmel Beach.

  • Jennifer Murphy, MPT

    Jennifer Murphy, MPT, graduated from Samuel Merritt College in 2000 with a Masters in Physical Therapy. Quickly thereafter, she enrolled in a year long intensive Manual Therapy course with John Seivert of Grass Valley. When Pilates became a critical adjunct in her practice, she completed her Pilates training and became certified by The Pilates Method Alliance in 2005.

    As an undergraduate, Jennifer received her Bachelors degree from Stanford University majoring in Human Biology. While at Stanford, Jennifer was co-captain of the Women’s Track and Field Team and earned two time All-American Honors. She now enjoys chasing her two small children while keeping balance between her Pilates practice, running and swimming.

  • Emily Penzes

    Emily Penzes was born and raised in Huntington Beach and attended college at California State University Chico.

    She joined Ronning Physical Therapy in 2010 as an administrative assistant/ scheduling coordinator. She is in charge of verifying insurance benefits and handling workers compensation authorization.

    She enjoys camping, traveling and live music.

  • Tiffany Hosick

    Tiffany joined Ronning Physical Therapy in 2001 as a PT aide/ front office assistant and in 2010 was promoted to office manager. She has 15 years of administrative experience working with numerous insurance companies, including private insurances, workers compensation and auto. She is in charge of patient accounts and billing.

    Tiffany is a Santa Cruz native; she enjoys Pilates, walking on West Cliff and traveling.
